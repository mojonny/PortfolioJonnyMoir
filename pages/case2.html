Creating this portfolio

I decided early on to learn both UX/UI Design along with Front-End Web Development. I thought that this would give me a fuller picture of what goes into designing apps and other interfaces that a user might interact with. I also couldn't resist learning more about the magic of the internet!

UX/UI Design of my site

So, using Figma and support from my Google UX Design course, I designed up to a hi-fi Prototype which is shared below. I found this experience to be a bit of a roller coaster as I was learning the skills to make the site work while also not really know what I needed to share.

Coding The Site

I am very proud of what I have accomplished in such a short time! Although the site still has a simplistic design and functionality, it is slowly starting to take on a more professional feel. I am hesitant to add many more animations and moving pieces as it reminds me of the days of doing junky school PowerPoints or the dizzying experiences of Prezi's.

Currently, the site is using mainly HTML and CSS to look this good. I do have some Javascript running for some interactive tables and such, but I want to make sure I fully understand my JS code before slapping it in here.
